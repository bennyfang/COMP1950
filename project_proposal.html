Proposal Theme

The theme of the project will be a blog style website that introduces authentic Japanese food. It will include several types of food commonly found in Japan as well as its characteristics, where you can find them, price, pictures, links to other sites about the food, etc. The website will be initially divided into big categories as main headings, then in each heading, with separate pages, specific food that belongs to its corresponding category will be discussed.


Audience Analysis

The target audience will be foodies as well as people who are specifically interested in Japanese culture and cuisine. They may or may not have prior knowledge about Japanese food, but the site will try to educate to both the experienced and the beginners about food beyond sushi and ramen which are currently popular. This site may also serve the needs of tourists who are planning to visit Japan but are not sure what to eat there or what to order at a specific style of restaurant (family restaurant, pub, sushi restaurant, etc.).


Pages to Include

  • Homepage
  • A page with links to posts as well as additional resources
  • 2 posts about Japanese restaurants and their food
  • About me
  • Contact me
  • Site map


Project Objectives

Primary Objectives:

  • Inform users about authentic Japanese food (and drinks, if included)
  • Promote Japanese food to more people globally
  • Provide detailed analysis of different food in terms of taste, price, where they are served, etc.
  • Increase my facebook and instagram traffics and likes

Secondary Objectives:

  • Promote interests in Japanese culture
  • Serve as food guide for people visiting Japan
  • Be referenced from another food blog post as additional readings
